What is Wiki Fetcher?

Wiki Fetcher is a smart learning tool that helps students read Wikipedia without distractions. It removes ads, sidebars, and clutter, letting you focus on learning.

Why Students Love Wiki Fetcher

  • Distraction-free Wikipedia reading
  • Paragraph-by-paragraph learning mode
  • Fast, lightweight, and privacy focused
  • Supports multiple languages
